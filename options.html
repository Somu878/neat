How Neat Sorts Tabs

1. Rules (always active): Neat sniffs tab URLs against 80+ known site patterns (GitHub, YouTube, Gmail, etc.). Matched tabs get categorized instantly with zero AI calls.

2. Chrome AI (for mystery snacks): Tabs that rules can't categorize are sent to Chrome's built-in AI model. This runs locally on your device - no data leaves your browser, no API key needed, zero cost.

3. Fallback: If Chrome AI isn't available, mystery tabs go to "Other" until Neat learns their flavor.
